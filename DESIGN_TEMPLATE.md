Refined Shopping Cart: A minimalist cart where each item is presented in the same card style as the home feed for total consistency.
Refined Checkout: A structured shipping and summary page with soft-bordered inputs and a prominent primary action button.
Refined Order Confirmation: A peaceful "thank you" screen featuring the warm orange checkmark and serif typography for a high-end feel.
Refined Account Access: A welcoming login and signup experience that uses the same background texture and typography as the rest of the app.
Refined My Orders List: A cohesive history screen where order cards match the visual language of the home feed's product cards.
Refined Order Details: A comprehensive breakdown of a single order, utilizing the established timeline and pricing styles.
Saved Wishlist: A dedicated space for users to track products they love, mirroring the home feed's elegant card style.
User Profile Hub: A centralized dashboard for managing account details, orders, and preferences with a clean, airy layout.
Shipping Addresses: A management screen for saved locations, optimized for the UAE context (Home, Villa, Office).
Search & Explore: A discovery-focused page featuring a search bar, recent history, and trending curated categories.
Empty Cart State: A thoughtful, visually pleasing "empty" state that encourages users to start shopping with a gentle nudge.
Activity Notifications: A minimalist list for delivery updates and price drops, designed to be helpful without being overwhelming.
Payment Options: A secure-feeling screen for managing cards and local payment methods like Apple Pay and Tabby.
